How It Works

From napkin sketch to
revenue-generating company

Four steps. No code required. No team to hire. Your AI CEO handles everything.

01

Describe Your Idea

Tell us what software product you want built. Target audience, problem it solves, rough concept. That is all the AI needs to get started.

02

AI Builds a Business Plan

Your CEO agent researches the market, validates demand, designs the product, and presents a full business plan for your approval.

03

Autonomous Development

The agent builds, deploys, and launches your product. It handles engineering, marketing, support, and payments -- autonomously and continuously.

04

Revenue Flows In

As customers pay, revenue is distributed automatically. You earn 15% as the founder. Investors and workers earn their shares too.
